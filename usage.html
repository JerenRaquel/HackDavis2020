Possible Energy Usages

Water

Water is an essential part of most peoplel's lifestyle as it is essential for hygene, cooking, and other activities. Showering and taking baths are common tasks that consume alot of water. In order to manage your usage, shorter showers are recommended and baths are discouraged if shorter showers are an option. Additionally, wasting water is discourged when over watering plants. Watering plants should only be done when neccassary and wasting water should be minimized to save on costs and to support the enviornment.

Electricity

Electricity is present with all the technology people may use or have access to. Lights, computer electronics, and machines such as washing machines or Heaters rely on the use of electricity. Minimizing the use of electricty would support the enviornment as less resources would be consumed and society in general would have access to more energy. Ways to manage electricty use is to be coutious of the devices that are plugged in and to turn of lights or machines that are not in use.

Heater

Heaters are an important part of most pepole's lives as it allows people to feel comfortable in their own homes. However, heaters consume a large amount of energy. To manage usage, the heater should be turned off when people are away.

Air Conditioner

Air conditioners consume large amounts of electricity. In order to save energy and manage usage, turn off the air conditioner when you are not present in your room. If that is unprefered, you may lower the settings of the air conditioner to keep the room cool to a reasonable level.
