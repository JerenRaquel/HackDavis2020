Watering plants should only be done when neccassary and wasating water shoudl be minimized to save on costs and to support the enviornment. Minimizing the use of electricty would support the enviornment as less resources would be consumed and society in general would have access to more energy. Ways to manage electricty use is to be coutious of the devices that are plugged in and to turn of lights or machines that are not in use.
